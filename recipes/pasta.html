Pasta

Description

Pasta is a staple of Italian cuisine, made from wheat flour and water, often served with various sauces.

Ingredients

  • pasta (spaghetti, penne, etc.)
  • olive oil
  • garlic
  • cherry tomatoes
  • fresh basil
  • Parmesan cheese
  • salt
  • pepper
  • red pepper flakes (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot and add salt.
  2. Add pasta and cook according to package instructions until al dente.
  3. While pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  4. Add minced garlic and sautÃ© until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  5. Add halved cherry tomatoes and cook until they start to soften, about 3-4 minutes.
  6. Drain the pasta, reserving a cup of pasta water.
  7. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with tomatoes and garlic.
  8. Mix well, adding reserved pasta water a little at a time to create a sauce.
  9. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes if desired.
  10. Remove from heat and stir in fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese.
  11. Serve immediately, garnished with extra Parmesan and basil if desired.
