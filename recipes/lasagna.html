Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, cheese, and meat sauce.

Ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • ground beef
  • ricotta cheese
  • mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • egg
  • marinara sauce
  • garlic
  • onion
  • olive oil
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and sauté chopped onion and minced garlic until translucent.
  4. Add ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  5. Stir in marinara sauce and let simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and pepper.
  7. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish.
  8. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese.
  9. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  10. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  11. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  12. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
